# Polymer Chemistry — Terminology & Controlled Vocabulary

## POL-T001

Term: Polymer

Definition: A substance composed of macromolecules characterized by the multiple repetition of one or more species of atoms or groups of atoms (repeat units) linked by covalent bonds.

Source: POL-001; POL-002

Status: Verified

## POL-T002

Term: Dispersity (PDI)

Definition: The ratio of weight-average molar mass to number-average molar mass ($M_w / M_n$), measuring the breadth of molecular weight distribution in a synthetic polymer.

Source: POL-001; POL-002

Status: Verified
