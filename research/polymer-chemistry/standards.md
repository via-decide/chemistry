# Polymer Chemistry — Standards

## IUPAC Purple Book

- Title: Compendium of Polymer Terminology and Nomenclature, IUPAC Recommendations 2008
- Publisher: IUPAC / RSC Publishing
- DOI: 10.1039/9781847559425
- Type: Nomenclature and terminology standard
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: IUPAC identifies the Purple Book as *Compendium of Polymer Terminology and Nomenclature, IUPAC Recommendations 2008*, prepared for publication by Richard G. Jones, Edward S. Wilks, W. Val Metanomski, Jaroslav Kahovec, Michael Hess, Robert Stepto, and Tatsuki Kitayama. [12](https://iupac.org/what-we-do/books/purplebook/)
- Evidence: IUPAC states that the Purple Book collects position papers on nomenclature and terminology for several types of polymers and that the 2nd edition expands and revises the 1991 edition. [12](https://iupac.org/what-we-do/books/purplebook/)
- Evidence: RSC states that the Purple Book covers terminology used in many aspects of polymer science and nomenclature for different types of polymer including regular and irregular single-strand organic polymers, copolymers, and regular double-strand organic polymers. [13](https://books.rsc.org/books/edited-volume/314/Compendium-of-Polymer-Terminology-and-Nomenclature)
