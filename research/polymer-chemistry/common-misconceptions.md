# Polymer Chemistry — Common Misconceptions

## POL-M001

Misconception: Synthetic polymers have a single, fixed molecular weight like small molecules.

Correction: Synthetic polymers consist of polydisperse mixtures of chains of varying lengths, requiring statistical averages ($M_n, M_w$) and dispersity $PDI$ to define molecular mass.
