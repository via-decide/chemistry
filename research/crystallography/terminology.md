# Crystallography — Terminology

## Terms

| Term | Controlled definition | Source | Boundary note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| direct lattice | Mathematical representation of the translational periodicity associated with an ideal periodic crystal structure | CRY-002 | It is not the finite physical crystal or its full atomic motif |
| unit cell | Parallelepiped constructed from a crystallographic basis | CRY-002 | Primitive and multiple cells contain different numbers of lattice points |
| reciprocal lattice | Integer combinations of reciprocal-basis vectors | CRY-003 | State whether the reciprocal convention includes `2\pi` |
| reciprocal-lattice vector | Vector associated with an indexed family of direct-lattice planes | CRY-003 | Its direction is normal to that plane family in the cited convention |
| interplanar spacing | Perpendicular spacing associated with an indexed family of lattice planes | CRY-003; CRY-004 | Do not confuse `d` with a generic atom-to-atom distance |
| Bragg reflection | Diffracted condition associated with a reciprocal-lattice node and indexed plane family | CRY-003 | Reflection position and intensity carry different structural constraints |
| structure determination | Process of establishing three-dimensional positional and usually displacement parameters for scattering centres | CRY-010 | Broader than refinement alone |
| refinement | Adjustment of a trial structure to improve agreement between observed and calculated diffraction measures | CRY-006 | Refinement is model fitting, not independent proof of correctness |
| checkCIF | IUCr service for consistency and integrity checks on CIF-reported structure determinations | CRY-007 | Validation alerts require scientific interpretation |
