# Crystallography — Common Misconceptions

## Misconception CRY-MC001

Misconception: The lattice is the physical crystal structure.

Correct explanation: The direct lattice is a mathematical representation of translational periodicity. A finite real crystal and its atomic pattern require additional structural information.

Evidence: CRY-002

Verification: Verified

Educational notes: Keep lattice nodes visually distinct from atoms or molecular motifs.

## Misconception CRY-MC002

Misconception: Refinement and structure solution are the same operation.

Correct explanation: Structure determination is the broader process. Refinement adjusts parameters of an approximate trial model to improve agreement with observed diffraction data.

Evidence: CRY-006; CRY-010

Verification: Verified

Educational notes: The workflow visual must show solution before refinement and validation after refinement.
