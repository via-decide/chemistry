# Crystallography — Standards

## IUCr Commission on Crystallographic Nomenclature

- Title: Commission on Crystallographic Nomenclature
- Publisher: International Union of Crystallography
- Type: Nomenclature governance source
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: IUCr states that nomenclature problems recognized as important by the Commission are studied by ad hoc committees of experts appointed by the Commission. [6](https://www.iucr.org/resources/commissions/crystallographic-nomenclature)
- Evidence: IUCr states that reports from these working groups, after acceptance by the Commission and Executive Committee, are published in *Acta Crystallographica*, Section A. [6](https://www.iucr.org/resources/commissions/crystallographic-nomenclature)
