# Chromatography — Research Queue & Gates

## CHR-RQ001

Target spread: S110 (pages 219–220) — Process Flow

Gate requirement: Freeze one verified chromatographic separation workflow (e.g., reversed-phase HPLC or capillary GC) showing sample injection, column partition, band migration, detector output, and fraction collection.

Status: Open

## CHR-RQ002

Target spread: S111 (pages 221–222) — Quantitative Walkthrough

Gate requirement: Independently fit and unit-check a published chromatogram dataset showing peak retention times $t_R$, baseline widths $W$, calculated $k$, $alpha$, $N$, $HETP$, $R_s$, and a Van Deemter curve.

Status: Open

## CHR-RQ003

Target spread: S113 (pages 223–224) — Instrument Equipment Cutaway

Gate requirement: Select one documented HPLC/UHPLC or GC system architecture (pumps/carrier, injector loop, column oven, flow cell / FID detector, dead volume connections).

Status: Open
