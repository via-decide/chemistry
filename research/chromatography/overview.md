# Chromatography — Overview Research

## Definition and scope

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: chromatography
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC defines chromatography as a physical method of separation in which components are distributed between two phases, one stationary and one mobile moving in a definite direction. [11](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/C01075)
