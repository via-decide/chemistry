## Core concepts

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: photochemistry
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC defines photochemistry as chemistry concerned with chemical effects of light. [8](https://old.goldbook.iupac.org/pdf/P04588.pdf)

- Source: IUPAC photochemistry glossary
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: The IUPAC glossary states that terms were added related to molecular anisotropy, polarized radiation, non-linear optical phenomena, and computation of excited species. [9](https://publications.iupac.org/pac/2007/pdf/7903x0293.pdf)

- Source: Chemical Reviews
  Publication: Introduction: Photochemical Catalytic Processes
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: ACS states that excited molecules can have chemical reactivity that differs from ground-state reactivity. [10](https://pubs.acs.org/doi/pdf/10.1021/acs.chemrev.1c00993?download=true)
