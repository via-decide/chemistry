# Photochemistry — Standards

## IUPAC Gold Book — photochemistry

- Title: photochemistry
- Publisher: IUPAC Gold Book
- DOI: 10.1351/goldbook.P04588
- Type: Terminology source
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: IUPAC defines photochemistry as the branch of chemistry concerned with the chemical effects of light, from far UV to IR. [7](https://old.goldbook.iupac.org/pdf/P04588.pdf)

## IUPAC Glossary of Terms Used in Photochemistry

- Title: Glossary of Terms Used in Photochemistry, 3rd edition
- Publisher: IUPAC
- Year: 2006 recommendation document shown in collected source
- Type: Terminology recommendation
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: The IUPAC glossary document states that the 3rd edition corrected and updated the 2nd edition and added terms related to molecular anisotropy, polarized radiation, non-linear optical phenomena, and computation of excited species. [8](https://kgroup.du.edu/resources/iupac.pdf)
