# Photochemistry — Overview

## Publication boundary

### Claim PHO-C001

Statement: Photochemistry is the branch of chemistry concerned with the chemical effects of light across the far-ultraviolet to infrared range in the IUPAC definition.

Evidence: PHO-001; PHO-002

Status: Verified

### Claim PHO-C002

Statement: A publishable photochemical account must identify the absorbing species, radiation field, prepared state or justified state manifold, competing photophysical processes, chemical event, environment, and evidence boundary rather than treating illumination alone as a mechanism.

Evidence: PHO-001; PHO-016; PHO-019

Status: Verified
