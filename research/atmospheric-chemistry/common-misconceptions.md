# Atmospheric Chemistry — Common Misconceptions

## ATM-M001

Misconception: Tropospheric ozone ($O_3$) is emitted directly by industrial smokestacks.

Correction: Ground-level tropospheric ozone is a secondary pollutant formed in the atmosphere via photochemical reactions between nitrogen oxides ($NO_x$), volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and sunlight.
