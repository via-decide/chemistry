# Chemical Engineering — Common Misconceptions

## CHE-M001

Misconception: Laboratory beaker kinetics can be scaled to commercial reactors by simply multiplying liquid volume.

Correction: Reactor scale-up introduces severe mass transfer, heat removal, mixing non-idealities, and wall effects that require dimensionless numbers ($Re, Nu, Sh$) and pilot plant validation.
