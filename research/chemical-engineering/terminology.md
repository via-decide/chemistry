# Chemical Engineering — Terminology & Controlled Vocabulary

## CHE-T001

Term: Chemical Engineering

Definition: The branch of engineering concerned with the design, construction, operation, optimization, and safety of industrial processes that transform raw materials into valuable products via chemical, physical, and biological transformations.

Source: CHE-001; CHE-002

Status: Verified

## CHE-T002

Term: Unit Operation

Definition: A basic step in a chemical process involving physical change, such as distillation, absorption, filtration, heat exchange, or drying.

Source: CHE-001; CHE-002

Status: Verified
