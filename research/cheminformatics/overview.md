# Cheminformatics — Overview Research

## Scope

- Chemical structure information.
- Chemical databases.
- Chemical identifiers.
- Structure search.
- Information retrieval.
- Data mining.
- Machine learning for chemical information.
- Digital chemistry standards.

  Publication: cheminformatics
  Confidence: High
