# Cheminformatics — Fundamentals Research

## Core concepts

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: cheminformatics
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC defines cheminformatics as involving chemical-structure-specific information used in data mining, information retrieval, information extraction, and machine learning. [1](https://sciop.net/datasets/nist-asd)

- Source: InChI Trust
  Publication: About the IUPAC InChI Standard
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: The InChI Trust states that InChI is a standard identifier for chemical databases that facilitates effective information management across chemistry. [2](https://nptel.ac.in/courses/104106089)

- Source: NIST Chemistry WebBook
  Publication: IUPAC Identifier Search
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: NIST states that its identifier search allows searching species data by IUPAC International Chemical Identifiers, including InChI and InChIKey. [4](https://onlinecourses.nptel.ac.in/noc25_cy46/preview)
