# Cheminformatics — Research Frontier

## Curated source anchors

### IUPAC cheminformatics term

- Title: cheminformatics
- Publisher: IUPAC Gold Book
- DOI: 10.1351/goldbook.11421
- Type: Terminology source
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: IUPAC explicitly connects cheminformatics to chemical-structure information, data mining, information retrieval, information extraction, and machine learning. [1](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/11421)
- Frontier theme: machine-readable chemical knowledge, chemical databases, machine learning.

### Cheminformatics data and standards

- Title: Cheminformatics: Data and Standards
- Publisher: Chemistry International / De Gruyter Brill
- Year: 2022
- Type: Standards overview
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: The article states that two longstanding IUPAC standards and recommendations relevant to cheminformatics include InChI for chemical identification and JCAMP-DX for spectral data exchange. [5](https://www.degruyterbrill.com/document/doi/10.1515/ci-2022-0324/html)
- Evidence: The article states that IUPAC digital standards and cheminformatics have been active areas of interest through IUPAC projects and the Subcommittee on Cheminformatics Data Standards. [5](https://www.degruyterbrill.com/document/doi/10.1515/ci-2022-0324/html)
