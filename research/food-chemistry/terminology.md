# Food Chemistry — Terminology & Controlled Vocabulary

## FOO-T001

Term: Food Chemistry

Definition: The study of chemical processes, composition, physical properties, and interactions of macro- (proteins, lipids, carbohydrates, water) and micro-components (vitamins, minerals, flavors, pigments) in food systems during processing and storage.

Source: FOO-001; FOO-002

Status: Verified

## FOO-T002

Term: Water Activity (aw)

Definition: The ratio of the vapor pressure of water in a food matrix to the vapor pressure of pure water at the same temperature, determining thermodynamic water availability for reactions and microbial growth.

Source: FOO-001

Status: Verified
