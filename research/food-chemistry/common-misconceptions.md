# Food Chemistry — Common Misconceptions

## FOO-M001

Misconception: Total percentage moisture content directly determines food spoilability and shelf life.

Correction: Two foods with identical 15% moisture content can have completely different stability if their water activity $a_w$ differs. $a_w$ dictates microbial growth thresholds (e.g., $a_w < 0.60$ prevents all microbial growth).
