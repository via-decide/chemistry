# Nuclear Chemistry — Mathematics

## Claims

### Claim NUC-C013

Claim: Activity is the number of nuclear decays in a small time interval divided by that interval and can be written as the negative time derivative of the number of radioactive nuclei.

Evidence: NUC-005

Verification: Verified

### Claim NUC-C014

Claim: The decay constant relates activity to the number of decaying nuclei through `A = \lambda N`.

Evidence: NUC-006

Verification: Verified

### Claim NUC-C015

Claim: For one exponential radioactive decay process, the half-life and decay constant are related by `t_{1/2} = \ln 2 / \lambda`.

Evidence: NUC-006; NUC-007; NUC-012

Verification: Verified

### Claim NUC-C016

Verification: Verified
