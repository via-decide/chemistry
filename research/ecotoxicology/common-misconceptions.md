# Ecotoxicology — Common Misconceptions

## ECO-M001

Misconception: Single-species lab $LC_{50}$ bioassays directly predict ecosystem-level impacts.

Correction: Single-species tests evaluate acute mortality under ideal lab conditions; real ecosystems feature species interactions, food-web biomagnification, chronic low-dose exposure, and multi-chemical mixtures.

Evidence: ECO-001; ECO-002
