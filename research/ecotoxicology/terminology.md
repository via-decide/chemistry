# Ecotoxicology — Terminology & Controlled Vocabulary

## ECO-T001

Term: Ecotoxicology

Definition: The branch of toxicology concerned with the study of toxic effects of chemical, physical, or biological agents on living organisms, especially on populations, communities, ecosystems, and the biosphere.

Source: ECO-001; ECO-002

Status: Verified

## ECO-T002

Term: PNEC (Predicted No-Effect Concentration)

Definition: The concentration of a chemical below which adverse effects on the environmental compartment are not expected to occur.

Source: ECO-001; ECO-002

Status: Verified
