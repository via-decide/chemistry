# Ecotoxicology — Standards

## IUPAC Glossary of Terms Used in Ecotoxicology

- Title: Glossary of Terms Used in Ecotoxicology
- Publisher: IUPAC / Pure and Applied Chemistry
- Year: 2009
- DOI: 10.1351/PAC-REC-08-07-09
- Type: Terminology recommendation
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: The IUPAC document identifies itself as *Glossary of Terms Used in Ecotoxicology (IUPAC Recommendations 2009)*. [10](https://media.iupac.org/publications/pac/2009/pdf/8105x0829.pdf)
- Evidence: The abstract states that the glossary aims to provide clear definitions for those contributing to ecotoxicology-related studies who are not themselves ecotoxicologists, especially chemists. [10](https://media.iupac.org/publications/pac/2009/pdf/8105x0829.pdf)
- Evidence: The abstract states that the glossary includes terms related to chemical speciation in the environment, sampling, monitoring, environmental analysis, adverse ecological effects of chemicals, ecological biomarkers, and environmental distribution of chemicals. [10](https://media.iupac.org/publications/pac/2009/pdf/8105x0829.pdf)
