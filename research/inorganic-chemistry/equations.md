# Inorganic Chemistry — Equations

## Equations

### Equation IC-EQ001

Equation: sum oxidation states = net charge

Variables: oxidation states; net charge

Units: dimensionless charge units

Assumptions: Assigned oxidation states under a stated convention.

Limitations: Formal oxidation states do not always equal real charges.

Applications: Oxidation-state accounting
