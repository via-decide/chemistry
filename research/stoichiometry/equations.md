# Stoichiometry — Equations

## Equations

### Equation ST-EQ001: mole-to-mole conversion

Equation: amount target = amount given × coefficient target / coefficient given

Variables: amount; stoichiometric coefficients

Units: mol; dimensionless coefficients

Assumptions: Balanced chemical equation.

Limitations: Only valid for the specified balanced reaction and extent assumptions.

Applications: Reaction calculations
