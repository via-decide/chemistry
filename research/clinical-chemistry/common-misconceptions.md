# Clinical Chemistry — Common Misconceptions

## CLN-M001

Misconception: An analytical lab result falling outside a 95% reference interval proves the patient has a pathology.

Correction: Statistically, 5% of completely healthy individuals naturally fall outside a 95% reference interval. Clinical diagnosis requires evaluating clinical decision limits and symptoms.
