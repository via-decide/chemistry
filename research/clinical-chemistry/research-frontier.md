# Clinical Chemistry — Research Frontier

## Curated source anchors

### IUPAC-IFCC clinical chemistry glossary

- Title: Glossary of Terms in Quantities and Units in Clinical Chemistry
- Authors: H. P. Lehmann; X. Fuentes-Arderiu; L. F. Bertello
- Journal: Pure and Applied Chemistry
- Year: 1996
- Type: Terminology recommendation
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: The PAC document identifies the glossary as an IUPAC-IFCC recommendation in Pure and Applied Chemistry, volume 68, issue 4, pages 957–1000. [8](https://old.iupac.org/publications/pac/1996/pdf/6804x0957.pdf)
- Frontier theme: clinical laboratory terminology, quantities, units, metrology.

### Clinical laboratory sciences compendium

- Title: Compendium of Terminology and Nomenclature of Properties in Clinical Laboratory Sciences: Recommendations 2016
- Publisher: Royal Society of Chemistry
- Year: 2016
- Type: Compendium
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: RSC states that the compendium groups and updates recommendations and is appropriate for laboratory scientists, medical professionals, and students. [9](https://books.rsc.org/books/monograph/1831/Compendium-of-Terminology-and-Nomenclature-of)
- Frontier theme: clinical data exchange, properties, units, standardized reporting.
