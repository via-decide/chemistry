# Clinical Chemistry — Standards

## IUPAC-IFCC Glossary of Terms in Quantities and Units in Clinical Chemistry

- Title: Glossary of Terms in Quantities and Units in Clinical Chemistry
- Publisher: IUPAC / IFCC
- Type: Terminology recommendation
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: The IUPAC page states that the glossary was prepared by the IUPAC-IFCC/C-NPU and based on a document published in *Biochimica Clinica* in 1995. [7](https://media.iupac.org/publications/labinfo/English/Documents/glossary.htm)
- Evidence: The introduction states that the goals of the IUPAC and IFCC bodies are to participate in defining quantities and units used for health care and related activities and to ensure consistency with standards from authoritative international metrology organizations. [7](https://media.iupac.org/publications/labinfo/English/Documents/glossary.htm)
