# Clinical Chemistry — Terminology & Controlled Vocabulary

## CLN-T001

Term: Clinical Chemistry

Definition: The area of clinical pathology concerned with qualitative and quantitative analysis of bodily fluids (blood, serum, plasma, urine, CSF) to aid disease diagnosis, prognosis, and therapeutic monitoring.

Source: CLN-001; CLN-002

Status: Verified

## CLN-T002

Term: Reference Interval

Definition: The statistical range of analyte values (typically central 95% interval) observed in a defined healthy reference population.

Source: CLN-001; CLN-002

Status: Verified
