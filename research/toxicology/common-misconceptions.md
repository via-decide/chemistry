# Toxicology — Common Misconceptions

## TOX-M001

Misconception: Synthetic chemicals are inherently more toxic than naturally occurring chemicals.

Correction: "The dose makes the poison" (Paracelsus). Toxicity is determined by chemical structure, dose, exposure route, and bioavailability, not by whether a molecule is synthetic or natural (e.g., botulinum toxin is natural yet lethal at sub-nanogram doses).
