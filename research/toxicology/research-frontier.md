### IUPAC toxicokinetics glossary

- Title: Glossary of Terms Used in Toxicokinetics
- Authors: Monica Nordberg; John H. Duffus; Douglas M. Templeton
- Journal: Pure and Applied Chemistry
- Year: 2004
- Type: Terminology recommendation
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: The IUPAC document identifies Monica Nordberg, John H. Duffus, and Douglas M. Templeton as preparers of the toxicokinetics glossary. [5](https://old.iupac.org/publications/pac/2004/pdf/7605x1033.pdf)
- Evidence: The abstract states that the glossary contains definitions of 365 toxicokinetics terms. [5](https://old.iupac.org/publications/pac/2004/pdf/7605x1033.pdf)
- Frontier theme: toxicokinetic terminology, hazard assessment, risk assessment.

### Fundamental Toxicology for Chemists

- Title: Fundamental Toxicology for Chemists
- Publisher: Royal Society of Chemistry
- Year: 1996 print; online publication page shown by RSC
- Type: Introductory toxicology text
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: RSC states that the book offers an assessment of toxicology specifically for chemists and covers areas including reproduction, behavioural, and ecological toxicology. [6](https://iupac.org/terminology-of-electroanalytical-methods-of-analysis/)
- Frontier theme: toxicology education for chemists, risk assessment, chemical safety.
