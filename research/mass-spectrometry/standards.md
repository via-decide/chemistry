# Mass Spectrometry — Standards

## IUPAC Gold Book — mass spectrometry

- Title: mass spectrometry
- Publisher: IUPAC Gold Book
- DOI: 10.1351/goldbook.M03746
- Type: Terminology source
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: IUPAC defines mass spectrometry as the branch of science dealing with all aspects of mass spectroscopes and the results obtained with these instruments. [1](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/5-60-thermodynamics-kinetics-spring-2008/pages/lecture-notes/)
- Evidence: IUPAC cites the Orange Book and the 1991 recommendations for nomenclature and symbolism for mass spectroscopy as sources for the term. [1](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/5-60-thermodynamics-kinetics-spring-2008/pages/lecture-notes/)
