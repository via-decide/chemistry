# Mass Spectrometry — Research Queue & Gates

## MS-RQ001

Target spread: S106 (pages 211–212) — Instrument Process Flow

Gate requirement: Freeze one verified mass-spectrometric workflow (e.g., LC-MS/MS or GC-EI-MS) showing exact stages from sample inlet through ionization, mass separation, and electron multiplier detection.

Status: Open

## MS-RQ002

Target spread: S107 (pages 213–214) — m/z and High-Resolution Spectra

Gate requirement: Independently calculate and verify an exact monoisotopic mass and isotope distribution pattern ($M, M+1, M+2$) for one benchmark compound at $R > 30{,}000$.

Status: Open
