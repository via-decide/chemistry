# Medicinal Chemistry — Research Frontier

## Curated source anchors

### IUPAC medicinal chemistry glossary

- Title: Glossary of Terms Used in Medicinal Chemistry
- Publisher: IUPAC
- Year: 1998
- Type: Terminology source
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: The IUPAC glossary page states that the definitions are identical to those in the published document in *Pure and Applied Chemistry*, volume 70, pages 1129–1143, 1998. [5](https://iupac.qmul.ac.uk/medchem/)
- Frontier theme: medicinal chemistry terminology, drug discovery language standardization.

### PubChem as compound-data anchor

- Title: PubChem
- Publisher: NCBI / NIH
- Type: Government chemical database
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: PubChem states that users can search chemicals by name, molecular formula, structure, and identifiers and find properties, biological activities, safety and toxicity information, patents, and literature citations. [6](https://ether.chem.iitb.ac.in/~bltembe/pdfs/ch_3.pdf)
- Frontier theme: compound data, bioactivity, safety, patent and literature links.
