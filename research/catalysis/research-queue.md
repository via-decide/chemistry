# Catalysis — Research Queue & Gates

## CAT-RQ001

Target spread: S093 (pages 185–186) — Catalytic Cycle

Gate requirement: Freeze one specific, fully characterized catalytic cycle (e.g., Haber–Bosch iron surface cycle or C–C cross-coupling organometallic cycle) with verified elementary reaction steps and energy profile.

Status: Open

## CAT-RQ002

Target spread: S094 (pages 187–188) — Catalytic Metrics (TON/TOF)

Gate requirement: Independently fit and unit-check a published catalytic dataset showing conversion, selectivity, TON, and TOF over a complete reaction time-course.

Status: Open

## CAT-RQ003

Target spread: S095 (pages 189–190) — Catalytic Reactor Cutaway

Gate requirement: Select one documented industrial or lab-scale catalytic reactor (fixed-bed or CSTR) with explicit temperature control, mass-transfer boundary, and sampling locations.

Status: Open

## CAT-RQ004

Target spread: S096 (pages 191–192) — Catalytic Platform Comparison

Gate requirement: Populate comparative engineering metrics across homogeneous, heterogeneous, enzyme, photo-, and electro-catalysts (phase, TOF range, operating temp, separation method, energy source).

Status: Open
