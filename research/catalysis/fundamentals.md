# Catalysis — Fundamentals Research

## Core concepts

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: catalyst
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC states that a catalyst increases reaction rate without modifying the overall standard Gibbs energy change. [21](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/C00876/pdf)

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: catalyst
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC states that homogeneous catalysis involves one phase, while heterogeneous catalysis occurs at or near an interface between phases. [21](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/C00876/pdf)

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: catalyst
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC states that catalysis brought about by one product of a reaction is called autocatalysis. [21](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/C00876/pdf)
