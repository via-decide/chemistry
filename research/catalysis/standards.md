# Catalysis — Standards

## IUPAC Gold Book — catalyst

- Title: catalyst
- Publisher: IUPAC Gold Book
- DOI: 10.1351/goldbook.C00876
- Type: Terminology source
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: IUPAC defines a catalyst as a substance that increases the rate of a reaction without modifying the overall standard Gibbs energy change in the reaction. [18](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/C00876/pdf)
- Evidence: IUPAC states that catalysis can be classified as homogeneous catalysis, where one phase is involved, and heterogeneous catalysis, where the reaction occurs at or near an interface between phases. [18](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/C00876/pdf)
