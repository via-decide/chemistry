# Catalysis — Overview Research

## Definition and scope

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: catalyst
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC defines a catalyst as a substance that increases the rate of a reaction without modifying the overall standard Gibbs energy change in the reaction. [21](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/C00876/pdf)

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book

  Confidence: High
