# Green Chemistry — Terminology & Controlled Vocabulary

## GRE-T001

Term: Green Chemistry

Definition: The design of chemical products and processes that reduce or eliminate the use and generation of hazardous substances throughout their lifecycle.

Source: GRE-001; GRE-002

Status: Verified

## GRE-T002

Term: Atom Economy

Definition: A metric evaluating synthetic efficiency by calculating the percentage of atom mass from starting reactants incorporated into the desired final product.

Source: GRE-001; GRE-002

Status: Verified
