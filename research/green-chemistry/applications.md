# Green Chemistry — Applications Research

## Applications

- Sustainable synthesis.
- Safer chemical design.
- Pharmaceutical process redesign.
- Polymer recycling.
- Catalysis.
- Environmental pollution prevention.
- Renewable feedstocks.
- Safer laboratory and industrial chemistry.

## Evidence cards

- Source: ACS
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: ACS states that the 12 Principles of Green Chemistry can be used as a framework for making a greener chemical, process, or product. [7](https://www.acs.org/green-chemistry-sustainability/principles/12-principles-of-green-chemistry.html)

- Source: Yale Center for Green Chemistry & Green Engineering
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: Yale states that chemical products should be designed to perform their desired function while minimizing toxicity. [8](https://greenchemistry.yale.edu/about/principles-green-chemistry)
