# Green Chemistry — Overview Research

## Definition and scope

- Source: Yale Center for Green Chemistry & Green Engineering
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: Yale defines green chemistry as the design of chemical products and processes that reduce or eliminate the use and generation of hazardous substances. [8](https://greenchemistry.yale.edu/about/principles-green-chemistry)

- Source: ACS
  Publication: 12 Principles of Green Chemistry
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: ACS states that the 12 principles provide a framework for making a greener chemical, process, or product. [7](https://www.acs.org/green-chemistry-sustainability/principles/12-principles-of-green-chemistry.html)

## Scope

- Waste prevention.
- Atom economy.
- Safer solvents.
- Catalysis.
