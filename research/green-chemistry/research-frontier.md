- Type: Perspective
- Reliability: High

- Evidence: The abstract states that the Perspective looks back at the original 12 Principles and asks what changes are needed after more than a quarter century. [9](https://pubs.rsc.org/gc/article/28/13/5606/317191/Modernizing-the-12-principles-of-green-chemistry)

### ACS principles framework

- Title: 12 Principles of Green Chemistry
- Publisher: American Chemical Society
- Type: Framework source
- Reliability: High
- Evidence: ACS lists the principles as prevention, atom economy, less hazardous chemical syntheses, designing safer chemicals, safer solvents and auxiliaries, design for energy efficiency, renewable feedstocks, reduce derivatives, catalysis, design for degradation, real-time analysis for pollution prevention, and inherently safer chemistry for accident prevention. [7](https://www.acs.org/green-chemistry-sustainability/principles/12-principles-of-green-chemistry.html)
- Frontier theme: sustainable chemical design and process improvement.
