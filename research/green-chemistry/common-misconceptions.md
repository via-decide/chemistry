# Green Chemistry — Common Misconceptions

## GRE-M001

Misconception: A chemical reaction with 99% chemical yield is automatically a "green" process.

Correction: High yield does not guarantee greenness. A reaction can have 99% yield but poor atom economy (producing massive stoichiometric byproduct waste) or require tons of toxic solvent per kg product.
