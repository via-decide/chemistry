# Solubility — Research Queue

## P0: S073 molecular and phase-cycle evidence

- Select one molecular-solute and one ionic-solid case with verified solid form, solvent, temperature, composition basis, and evaluated solubility data.
- Source the lattice or intermolecular-interaction representation at figure level; the current sources do not support a quantitative lattice-versus-solvation energy diagram.
- Define the visual boundary between equilibrium phase return and kinetic nucleation or crystal growth.

Source boundary: SOL-001; SOL-002; SOL-003; SOL-008; SOL-009

## P0: S074 coupled-equilibrium worked system

- Select a salt whose thermodynamic constants, activity model, acid–base reactions, complexes, and temperature dependence are all traceable to one frozen database version.
- Preserve reaction conventions, standard states, charge balance, component mass balances, and uncertainty.
- Independently recompute the worked example and prohibit the idealized molar-solubility shortcut outside SOL-EQ006 assumptions.

Source boundary: SOL-004; SOL-005; SOL-006; SOL-007; SOL-010

## P0: S075 measurement protocol

- Choose whether the atlas shows the OECD column-elution or flask method; do not merge both into an impossible apparatus.
- Add method-specific analytical validation, blank, replicate, phase-separation, recovery, uncertainty, and post-equilibration solid-form checks.
- Source a second method for ionizable, reactive, multicomponent, or pharmaceutical systems if the selected example exceeds OECD TG 105 scope.

Source boundary: SOL-009; SOL-011

## P0: S076 crystallization-route decision tree

- Add figure-level evidence for cooling, evaporation, antisolvent, pH or reaction precipitation, and seeded branches.
- Declare solvent-safety, residual-solvent, heat-transfer, mixing, scale-up, filtration, drying, recycle, and waste boundaries.
- Choose one open, non-proprietary worked system or mark quantitative setpoints unavailable.
- Separate solid-form screening and regulatory control from process optimization.

Source boundary: SOL-012; SOL-013; SOL-014; SOL-015; SOL-016

## P1: Data and publication controls

- Freeze every IUPAC–NIST dataset retrieval, system identifier, units, transformations, checksum, and license.
- Verify current editions and jurisdictional applicability at final-copy freeze.
- Add textbook support only where it supplies a necessary worked derivation not already covered by an authoritative source; never use it to overwrite the thermodynamic convention.
- Commission no final illustration until labels, equation records, and source locators pass scientific review.
