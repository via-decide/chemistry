These packets establish authoritative evidence coverage for the planned spreads. Packet verification does not promote a spread to final illustration or copy status.

## Packet SOL-SP-S073

Spread: S073, pages 145–146

Visual subject: Solid or separate solute phase, solvent, dissolution and solvation boundary, saturated solution, supersaturation boundary, nucleation or precipitation return, and recovered solid phase.

Authoritative sources: SOL-001; SOL-002; SOL-003; SOL-008; SOL-009

Supported claims: SOL-C001; SOL-C002; SOL-C003; SOL-C004; SOL-C005; SOL-C009; SOL-C010

Required visual distinctions: equilibrium solubility versus dissolution rate; molecular or ionic species versus formula-unit shorthand; homogeneous solution versus dispersed solid; saturation versus supersaturation; precipitation potential versus observed nucleation; initial versus recovered solid form.

Data requirement: Freeze one evaluated solute–solvent system, phase identity, temperature, composition basis, source record, transformations, and uncertainty before drawing a quantitative curve or particle count.

Known limitation: The verified sources establish terminology and evaluated-data provenance but do not support a universal lattice-energy-versus-solvation-energy balance or one cycle that behaves identically for molecular, ionic, gaseous, and liquid solutes.
