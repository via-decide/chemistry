Atlas use: Supports the S076 requirement to treat solvent selection, process conditions, and polymorphic outcome as connected decisions.

## SOL-LIT002: Direct antisolvent concentration control

Record: SOL-016

Context: Primary research demonstrating a concentration-based supersaturation-control approach for a specific pharmaceutical antisolvent crystallization system.

Atlas use: Supports a process-control branch and callouts for concentration measurement, solubility model, supersaturation setpoint, manipulated input, and product boundary.

Exclusions: The proprietary-compound case does not establish transferable kinetics, solvent choice, seeding policy, mixing scale, or polymorph outcome for other systems.

## SOL-LIT003: Evaluated solubility-data method

Record: SOL-009

Context: IUPAC technical report explaining how experimental solubility literature is compiled, assessed, and converted into evaluated or recommended data.

Atlas use: Supports evidence-quality and data-provenance callouts for S073–S075.

Exclusions: It is a data-evaluation method, not a measurement result for a selected solute–solvent system.
