## Application domains

- Gas and liquid adsorption separations.
- Catalyst and porous-material characterization.
- Surface-sensitive chemical analysis.
- Adhesion, coatings, corrosion protection, and maintenance.
- Semiconductor, battery, sensor, and biomaterial interfaces.

These domains are contexts for case selection, not evidence for one transferable isotherm, instrument, coating system, or performance claim.
