## Definition and scope

- Source: IUPAC Gold Book
  Publication: adsorption
  Confidence: High
  Evidence: IUPAC defines adsorption as an increase in the concentration of a dissolved substance at the interface of a condensed and liquid phase due to surface forces. [13](https://goldbook.iupac.org/terms/view/A00155)
