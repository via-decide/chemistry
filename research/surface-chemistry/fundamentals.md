# Surface Chemistry — Fundamentals

### Claim SUR-C003

Statement: The Gibbs dividing surface is a geometrical reference used to allocate bulk and surface quantities; it must not be drawn as proof that a real interface has zero thickness.

Evidence: SUR-001; SUR-003

Status: Verified

### Claim SUR-C004

Statement: Adsorption is interfacial enrichment relative to declared bulk reference phases, whereas absorption refers to entry into the bulk; both may occur in one system.

Evidence: SUR-001; SUR-002; SUR-003; SUR-009

Status: Verified

### Claim SUR-C005

Statement: Surface excess is the difference between the actual amount of a component and the amount assigned to extrapolated bulk phases up to a chosen dividing surface.

Evidence: SUR-001; SUR-003

Status: Verified

### Claim SUR-C006

Statement: Surface excess concentration divides a declared surface excess by the area of the corresponding dividing surface or interface, so the area convention is part of the result.

Evidence: SUR-001; SUR-004

Status: Verified

### Claim SUR-C007

Statement: Surface coverage compares the adsorbed number or amount with the number or amount assigned to a filled monolayer under a declared convention.

Evidence: SUR-001; SUR-006; SUR-009

Status: Verified

### Claim SUR-C008

Statement: An adsorption or surface-excess isotherm relates an interfacial amount to pressure or composition at constant declared conditions; the measured relation and a fitted mechanistic model are different evidence objects.

Evidence: SUR-001; SUR-005; SUR-009

Status: Verified

### Claim SUR-C009

Statement: Chemisorption is defined through chemical-bond formation between adsorbent and adsorbate; strength, stoichiometry, site identity, reversibility, and activation barrier remain system-specific.

Evidence: SUR-008

Status: Verified

### Claim SUR-C010

Statement: Gas physisorption in porous solids can involve monolayer adsorption, multilayer adsorption, micropore filling, and pore condensation, which must not be collapsed into one generic surface-layer picture.

Evidence: SUR-001; SUR-009

Status: Verified
