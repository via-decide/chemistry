# Illustration Specification

## House illustration language

- Style: precise editorial vector with restrained 3D only where geometry or apparatus requires it; matte print palette; no photorealistic molecular fantasy.
- Lines: primary boundary 1.2 pt, secondary 0.7 pt, leader 0.5 pt at final size; minimum reversed line 0.75 pt.
- Labels: sentence case; SI symbols preserved; chemical formulas typeset; no label smaller than 7.5 pt at final print size.
- Scale: explicitly marked as true scale, relative scale, or schematic. Different scales cannot share one axis without a break.
- Molecules: atom identity, formal charge, stereochemistry, bond order, coordination, and phase must be checkable from the source packet.
- Equipment: show only connections, sensors, utilities, safeguards, and flow directions supported by the selected process boundary.
- Accessibility: color is never the sole encoding; pair color with shape, line pattern, sign, or label.
