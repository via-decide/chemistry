## Chapter production invariant

Every topic receives exactly four spreads in the same sequence:

1. Core system: scope, controlled vocabulary, and the dominant structural model.
2. Quantitative model: variables, equations, assumptions, units, and limitations.
3. Measurement to industry: apparatus or process boundary, controls, and industrial example.
4. Misconception, history, and frontier: model correction, contextualized research, and unresolved evidence.

Equal allocation does not imply equal readiness. `Verified`, `Needs Research`, and `Missing` gates in the page map determine whether a spread may enter design.
